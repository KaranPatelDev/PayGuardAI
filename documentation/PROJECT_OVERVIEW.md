# Project Overview

## What the Product Is
PayGuard AI is an invoice recovery and payment follow-up automation platform. It helps a business maintain customer records, create invoices, track unpaid amounts, monitor overdue invoices, record payments, generate AI follow-up messages, and review cashflow and recovery insights.

## Why It Was Built
Many businesses sell on credit but do not have a reliable receivables workflow. Payment follow-up often depends on manual calls, spreadsheets, notebooks, or scattered messages. This creates delays, missed reminders, and weak visibility into how much money is stuck.

## Who It Is Built For
The product is built for SMEs, vendors, agencies, wholesalers, consultants, distributors, local traders, manufacturers, service providers, and B2B companies that need to track pending payments without using complex enterprise finance software.

## Real-World Business Problem
When invoices are unpaid, owners often need to check due dates, identify overdue customers, message or call clients, remember promises, record partial payments, and understand expected cashflow. Doing this manually becomes difficult as customer count and invoice volume grow.

## Manual Invoice Recovery Today
A common manual process looks like this:

1. Invoice is created in accounting software, Excel, or PDF.
2. Owner or staff member checks due date manually.
3. Reminder is sent through WhatsApp, email, or phone.
4. Customer response is remembered informally or written in notes.
5. Payment status is updated later, often with delays.
6. Overdue customers are reviewed only when cashflow becomes tight.

## Why Manual Recovery Is Inefficient
- Follow-ups are easy to miss.
- Customer promises are not centrally tracked.
- Owners do not always know the current pending amount.
- Staff spend time calling and checking records repeatedly.
- Cashflow forecasts are weak because receivables are not organized.
- Business decisions are made without clear recovery data.

## How This Platform Improves the Process
PayGuard AI centralizes customers, invoices, payments, and follow-ups. It calculates invoice status from due dates and pending amounts, shows dashboard KPIs, records customer responses, generates AI follow-up messages, and produces recovery reports. This gives the business a repeatable process rather than a memory-based process.

## Usefulness in the Indian Market
Indian SMEs often work with credit cycles, GST records, UPI payments, distributor networks, and relationship-based collections. A lightweight invoice recovery tool can help business owners follow up professionally while preserving customer relationships and improving cashflow discipline.

## Business Segments That Benefit
- SMEs can track all receivables from a central dashboard.
- Vendors can monitor customer-wise pending amounts.
- Agencies and consultants can reduce follow-up friction with professional messages.
- Wholesalers and distributors can manage many retailers and overdue bills.
- Service businesses can track recurring unpaid invoices.
- B2B companies can improve collection predictability.
