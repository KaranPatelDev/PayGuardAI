# FAQ

## What is this product?
It is an invoice recovery and payment follow-up platform that helps businesses track pending invoices, manage follow-ups, record payments, and improve cashflow visibility.

## Who can use it?
Small businesses, wholesalers, distributors, vendors, agencies, consultants, accountants, service providers, and B2B companies.

## Is this only for large companies?
No. It is especially useful for small and medium businesses that need better payment tracking without enterprise complexity.

## How does it help small businesses?
It shows pending invoices, overdue amounts, customer risk, payments, and follow-up status in one place.

## Does it recover payments automatically?
No software can guarantee recovery. This platform helps organize and accelerate the follow-up process. Automated external reminders are a recommended future feature.

## Can it send reminders?
The current app can generate and record follow-up messages. Fully automated WhatsApp, SMS, and email sending are recommended future enhancements.

## Can I track overdue invoices?
Yes. The system calculates overdue status and overdue days for invoices with pending amounts.

## Can I manage multiple clients?
Yes. The Customers module lets you manage many clients and view their invoices, payments, follow-ups, and risk.

## Is my data safe?
The app uses JWT authentication and bcrypt password hashing. Production deployments should also use HTTPS, strong secrets, secure database access, backups, and audit logs.

## Can this be integrated with WhatsApp or email?
Yes, this is a recommended future integration.

## Can accountants use it?
Yes. Accountants can track receivables, payment records, overdue invoices, and recovery reports for businesses.

## Can wholesalers or distributors use it?
Yes. It is well suited for businesses that manage many retailers or credit customers.

## What features are planned next?
Recommended next features include WhatsApp/email/SMS reminders, UPI/Razorpay payment links, Excel imports, role-based access, audit logs, and advanced analytics.
