## Pricing Rationale

### Why does AI cost extra?

- **LLM API costs** — Each AI follow-up uses Claude Sonnet via the Emergent API
- **Compute** — Risk scoring, recovery reports, and invoice parsing require processing
- **Infrastructure** — Database, hosting, and email delivery have real costs

### How we keep costs low

- **Rule-based fallback** — When AI is unavailable, a deterministic template engine handles follow-ups
- **Caching** — Repeated queries are cached to reduce API calls
- **Serverless architecture** — Pay only for what you use

## FAQ

<details>
<summary><strong>Can I switch plans at any time?</strong></summary>

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade anytime. When upgrading, you're charged a prorated amount for the remaining billing period. Downgrade takes effect at the next billing cycle.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Is there a free trial for paid plans?</strong></summary>

Yes. All paid plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>What happens if I exceed my plan limits?</strong></summary>

You'll receive a notification when you reach 80% of your limit. At 100%, new AI follow-ups are paused until the next billing cycle or you upgrade. Invoices and customers are never restricted.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or early-stage startups?</strong></summary>

Yes. Contact [support@payguard.ai](mailto:support@payguard.ai) with your organization details.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>What payment methods do you accept?</strong></summary>

UPI, net banking, credit/debit cards, and international wire transfers for Enterprise plans.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Can I get a refund?</strong></summary>

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. Contact support within 7 days of your first purchase.
</details>
