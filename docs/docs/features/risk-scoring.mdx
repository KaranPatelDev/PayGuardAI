## Overview

Customer Risk Scoring assigns a **0-100 risk score** to each customer based on their payment behavior. The score auto-refreshes when invoices, payments, or follow-ups change. AI generates plain-language risk summaries for quick assessment.

### Risk Calculation Factors

| Factor | Weight | Description |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Payment Delays | 40% | Average days past due across all invoices |
| Broken Promises | 30% | Number of missed payment commitments |
| Disputed Invoices | 20% | Count and value of disputed invoices |
| Follow-up History | 10% | Number of follow-ups required |

### Risk Categories

| Category | Score Range | Action |
|----------|-------------|--------|
| Low Risk | 0 - 25 | Standard follow-up, maintain relationship |
| Medium Risk | 26 - 50 | Increased monitoring, regular reminders |
| High Risk | 51 - 75 | Escalated follow-ups, consider credit limits |
| Critical Risk | 76 - 100 | Immediate action, legal consultation recommended |

### Auto-Refresh Triggers

- New invoice created
- Payment recorded
- Follow-up sent or completed
- Invoice status changed
- Dispute raised or resolved

---

## How It Works

1. Risk score is calculated on first invoice creation
2. Each event (payment, follow-up, dispute) triggers a recalculation
3. The score updates in real-time across all views
4. AI generates a human-readable risk summary on demand

---

## API Endpoints

| Method | Endpoint | Description |
|--------|----------|-------------|
| GET | `/api/customers/{cid}/risk` | Get detailed risk assessment for a customer |
| POST | `/api/ai/generate-risk-summary` | Generate AI-powered risk summary |

:::tip
Review High and Critical risk customers weekly. Early intervention significantly improves recovery rates.
:::

:::note
Risk scores are normalized across all customers. Adding new customers may shift existing scores slightly.
:::

:::caution
Do not rely solely on risk scores for credit decisions. Use them as one input alongside manual review and business judgment.
:::
