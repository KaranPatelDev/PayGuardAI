# Welcome to PayGuard AI Documentation

**AI-powered payment recovery platform for Indian MSMEs**

PayGuard AI helps small and medium businesses in India manage invoices, track payments, and leverage AI to follow up on overdue payments — improving cash flow and reducing bad debt.
