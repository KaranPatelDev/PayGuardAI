# PayGuard AI Pricing Plan

## Positioning

PayGuard AI should be priced as an AI payment recovery and cashflow assistant for Indian MSMEs, traders, agencies, freelancers, wholesalers, and manufacturers.

It should not be priced like full accounting software. The strongest angle is:

> Recover pending payments faster, reduce manual follow-ups, and improve cashflow without hiring a collection person.

For Vadodara/Gujarat MSME customers, the pricing should feel affordable compared to:

- A part-time accountant or collection executive
- Lost cashflow from delayed invoices
- Time spent manually following up on WhatsApp, email, and calls
- Existing accounting software add-ons

## Recommended Pricing Strategy

Use monthly SaaS pricing with annual discounts.

Best starting approach:

- 14-day free trial
- No credit card required
- Monthly billing available
- 2 months free on annual plans
- Optional onboarding/setup fee for businesses that need help importing customers and invoices

## Recommended Final Pricing Page Layout

Show only 3 main plans publicly:

1. Starter: ₹699/month
2. Growth: ₹1,499/month - Most Popular
3. Pro: ₹3,499/month

Keep Business Plus and Enterprise as "Talk to Sales".

Reason:

Too many visible plans can confuse small business buyers. Three plans are easier to compare and easier to sell.

## Best Price To Start Selling

If selling manually in Vadodara, start with:

- ₹999/month introductory price for Growth

After getting 10 paying customers, move pricing to:

- Starter: ₹699/month

## Value-Based Pricing Angle

Use this simple ROI pitch:

If PayGuard helps recover even one delayed invoice of ₹25,000 faster, the monthly fee is already justified.

For MSMEs with ₹2 lakh to ₹20 lakh pending receivables, ₹1,499/month is a reasonable price if the product saves follow-up time and improves collection discipline.

## Pricing Notes

- Keep GST separate if registered.
- Mention "Prices exclusive of GST" on the pricing page.
- Offer annual plans aggressively because SaaS churn can be high in MSME markets.
- Do not start too cheap below ₹499/month because support and onboarding will become unprofitable.
- Do not charge transaction fees unless you directly process payments.
- If payment links are added later, pass payment gateway charges separately.

## Market Reference Notes

- Zoho Books India pricing starts at around ₹749/month when billed annually for its Standard plan and goes up across Professional, Premium, Elite, and Ultimate plans.
- Razorpay publicly positions payment gateway pricing around 2% + GST per transaction, with custom pricing for higher monthly volume.
- PayGuard AI should sit below full accounting suites for entry plans, but charge more for recovery intelligence, AI reports, and cashflow-focused workflows.
